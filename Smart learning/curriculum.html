SMART LEARNING Curriculum

UK Curriculum Support

Key Stage 1 (KS1)

Primary level learning for ages 5–7 focusing on basic Mathematics, English reading, writing, phonics, and foundational Science concepts.

Key Stage 2 (KS2)

Students aged 7–11 develop deeper understanding of arithmetic, grammar, comprehension, and problem solving.

Key Stage 3 (KS3)

Students aged 11–14 prepare for secondary education with advanced Maths, English literature, Science, and Computer Science.

GCSE Preparation

Our tutors help students prepare for GCSE exams with: • exam practice • past paper solving • concept clarity • revision strategies

Australian Curriculum Support

Primary School (Years 1–6)

Focus on building fundamental literacy and numeracy skills, including reading comprehension, writing, arithmetic, and science inquiry.

Secondary School (Years 7–10)

Students learn advanced concepts in Mathematics, English, Science, and Digital Technologies aligned with the Australian national curriculum.

Senior Secondary Support
